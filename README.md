### Overview  
A statistical exploration of socioeconomic and regional determinants of happiness across the UK, using regression modeling to understand predictors of well-being and subjective life satisfaction.

### Key Insights  
- Modeled **1,000+ survey records** to quantify relationships between happiness, life satisfaction, and regional differences.
- Applied OLS regression and found a significant positive relationship (b = 0.44, p = 0.002) even after controlling for income, employment, region, and social exclusion.
